Careers About Cache Insights

Consult

Consultation services provides pathways to create solutions to your challenges via established knowledge base, industry accepted practices, Technical Know – how. Strategizing resources to reduce capex & opex.

Build

Building a DataCenter comprises of procurement of requisite infrastructure and initiating services, which are necessary for the DataCenter architecture to perform at highest potential using DCIM Capabilities, reducing opex costs

Migration

Datacenter Migration enables an organization to upgrade its IT resources to another architecture either to upgrade or change location, reducing operational expenditure in process.

Backup

In case of any failures, it is imperative that business sensitive data is always protected and available for correct decisions.

Deploy

Deploying a DataCenter comprises of using the facility infrastructure (Storage, Network & Security) effectively to share the workload amongst the different hardware to ensure redundancy, sustainable power utilization, increase uptime.

Design

DataCenter Design serves as main chess-piece of DataCenter, Location of different infrastructure in the facility to maximize processing; placement of cooling units to keep facility at conducive temperature and Backup solutions to ensure availability in case of power failure creating a better DataCenter architecture through essential DCIM.

SUPPORT SERVICES

Technical support services for data centres help to address challenges with the servers, storage, software and networking equipment that constitute a data center, or the related processes involved in managing the equipment.

MANAGED DATACENTER SERVICES

Services range from hosting, managing and maintaining an entire data center to more discrete tasks such as upgrading servers or backing up data.

APPLICATION SERVICES

Application service provider is a large segment that provides software-based services to other companies to access technical services over a network to enhance DCIM capabilities

TECHNICAL TRAINING SERVICES

Technical consulting services, provide knowledge, skills and competencies that apply to hardware, software or processes related to managing a datacenter, or fixing, updating, integrating or managing any of the equipment within a data center.

TECHNICAL CONSULTING SERVICES

Consulting services provide expertise to help organizations make strategic decisions. Electing or designing new technology, redesigning existing technology, migrating existing technology to a new environment

FINANCING & LEASING SERVICES

Financing and leasing services can decrease entity Capex through leasing a datacenter facility; leasing equipment or financing a data center project, such as building or upgrading a data center facility.
